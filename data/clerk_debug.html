      Request the addition of a respondent
      • Any person has the right to request the identity of a respondent against whom a final judgement for an injunction for the protection of a minor be added to the searchable database, unless the respondent is a minor. The identity of each respondent is available for search by the general public.
      • Such a request can be made in writing and delivered by mail, facsimile, email or in person at any of the three Hillsborough Clerk of Court & Comptroller Office locations.
